EDAPHIC RESOURCES

Edaphic resources

Edaphic characteristics include such factors as water content, acidity, aeration, and the availability of nutrients. Influenced by factors inherent in the soil rather than by climatic factors.
Layers of soil is 6 horizon
1. O-horizon(organic zone)
2. A-horizon(top soil)
3. B-horizon (sub soil)
4. C-horizon(weathering rock)
5. D-horizon(weathered or decomposed rock)
6. R-horizon(bed rock)

EXAMPLE OF SOIL
Sandy soil- Sandy Soil is light, warm, dry and tend to be acidic and low in nutrients. Sandy soils are often known as light soils due to their high proportion of sand and little clay (clay weighs more than sand).
